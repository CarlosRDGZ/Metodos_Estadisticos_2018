$$\sigma^{2} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{\mathit{n}} x_{i}^{2} - \mathit{n}\bar{x}^{2}}{\mathit{n} - 1}$$

% Desviasion Estandar
$$\sigma_{\bar{x}} = \sqrt{\frac{\sigma^{2}}{n}}$$
